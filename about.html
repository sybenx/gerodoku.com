About GeruDoku

Shadow of Israphel

GeruDoku gained popularity through its use in the Yogscast's iconic Minecraft series, "Shadow of Israphel". This beloved series combined Minecraft gameplay with storytelling, creating a unique and immersive experience for viewers.

You can revisit the nostalgia by watching the Shadow of Israphel Minecraft Series on YouTube.

Maintaining GeruDoku

GeruDoku is currently maintained to work with modern versions of Minecraft while staying true to the original style used in the Shadow of Israphel series. The goal is to preserve this piece of Minecraft history for future generations of players.

If you're interested in contributing to the development of GeruDoku, please visit our GitHub repository.
